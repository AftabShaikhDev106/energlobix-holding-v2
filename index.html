Our Business Areas

EnerGlobix Energy

A global energy and commodity company focused on reliable supply,
distribution and strategic energy solutions.
EnerGlobix Energy operates across sectors connected to fuel,
commodities and infrastructure, supporting industries and markets
through strong international networks, operational expertise and
longterm partnerships.

EnerGlobix Agriculture

Global sourcing and distribution of agricultural commodities including
fertilizer, grain, wheat and cooking oil.
To support supply chains and international trade through reliable
sourcing, logistics and commodity partnerships across global food and
agricultural markets.

EnerGlobix Development

Residential, commercial and mixed-use developments across the UAE.
Focused on quality, location and long-term relevance, EnerGlobix
Development creates environments designed for modern living,
business growth and connected urban communities.
